ABOUT ME

LANGUAGE COACH

I’m proudly a non-native English teacher with over 10 years of experience in international, multilingual classrooms.

Why proudly? Because I know what learning English really feels like. I’ve been stuck on the infamous intermediate plateau, and I’ve fought my way through it. I know how frustrating it can be, and I also know how to help you move beyond it.

I’ve experienced the joy of becoming fluent, of connecting across cultures, and of unlocking new worlds through language. (I even taught myself Turkish!)

I’m a CELTA-certified teacher with an International Teaching Qualification, and I hold a Master’s degree in Educational Studies from KU Leuven.

I believe everyone can learn, with the right support, the right approach, and the right mindset.

I’m here to help you unlock the doors you once thought were closed!

But teaching is not the only thing I do! I am a researcher, writer, and EU-project contributor too.
